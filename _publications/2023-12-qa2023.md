In recent years, we&apos;ve seen remarkable advancements in natural language understanding, driven by machine learning. One key area benefiting from these advancements is question answering, which enhances user experience on search engines and social media platforms. Given that Arabic, the language of the Holy Qur&apos;an, is spoken by 1.8 billion people worldwide, it&apos;s crucial to develop effective NLP solutions for this complex language.
During the OSACT5 Qur&apos;an QA 2022 Shared Task, we tackled this challenge by developing an ensemble learning model based on Arabic variants of BERT models. To boost accuracy, we also implemented advanced post-processing techniques. Our efforts resulted in a Partial Reciprocal Rank (pRR) score of 56.6% on the official test set, showcasing the potential of our approach in handling Arabic for question answering tasks.
